Overview:

Each week of Learning to Invest focuses extensively on topics that beginning investors
need to know before beginning to trade. Week one, the most introductory week of these,
gets you familiar with stock graphs and the terms associated with them, as well as
introduces a beginners level trading strategy that can be used immediately after reading!

Learning to invest in total is a 4-Week self taught program that is as comprehensive as it is inexpensive.

What is Covered in Each Week?

Week 1

-Goals of investors & FAQ
-Key Terms to know starting out
-Options when buying stocks
-When to buy into stocks & introducing Support
-When to sell stocks & introducing Resistance
-Interpreting Stock Line Graphs
-Support and Resistance Investing Strategy

Week 2

-Dividend Investing Strategy
-Growth Investing
-What to look for in companies
-Dollar Cost Averaging
-Interpreting Candlestick graphs
-Uptrend Technical Indicators
-Downtrend Technical Indicators
